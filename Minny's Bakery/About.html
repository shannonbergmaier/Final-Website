OUR STORY

Minny’s Bakery first started by Minny Wilson herself. Her passion was baking and she shared that love with everyone including me. As she has passed I aim to carry on her legacy and bake love into every dessert just as Minny, my grandmom did.
My grandma Minny moved to Lancaster, PA when she was 22 years old to start up her pie business. She loved perfecting her craft and sharing with the community. Minny’s Bakery aims to carry on the traditions that Minny left us, sharing kindness, friendship, and most importantly, a tasty treat!

Minny’s Bakery is located on Queen Street in Lancaster City. Stop in to say hello and grab our famous chocolate pie while you’re at it!
